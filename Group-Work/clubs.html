Afterschool Academic Teams:

image

Debate Team: This team is a great way to utilize critical thinking and public speaking skills. You can learn and compete in various debate formats including public form, congress, imprompute and individual speaking. Debate Team meets twice a week (Monday & Wednesday) during the Fall and Winter trimesters.

image

Robotics Team: This team allows you to work efficiently with your teammates to build and code your own robot. You will compete in the FTC (First Tech Competition) with your teammates. Robotics Team meets twices a week (Thursdays & Fridays) during the Fall and Winter trimesters.

image

Chess Team: This team allows you to connect with other classmates who have a similar interest of playing chess. The chess team meets on Mondays afterschool and Thursdays during clubs time all year long.
