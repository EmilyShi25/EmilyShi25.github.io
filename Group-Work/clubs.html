Here, you get to know more about all the Academic Teams and clubs EPS has to offer! EPS provides great experiences for all interests and skills!

Middle band clubs:

These clubs meet weekly during Thursdays from 11:10-11:45. They are a great way for students to pursue and be involved in something they are passionate about. Below are just some of the most popular and favorited clubs from the 2021-2022 school year.

AQUILA Club: This is a literary magazine club showcasing student poetry, stories, song-writing and more creative pieces.

Eagle Eye Club: This is a newspaper club which writes on current issues educating our school community.

`

APIDA Club: Apida stands for Asian, Pacific-Islander Desi American club. The plan events such as culture nights celebrating the cultures and ethniticites of EPS students.

Business Club: This clubs brings in various guest speakers who are entrepeneurs so they can learn more about the journey of starting a business.

Environmental Club: This club focuses on helping the EPS community by raising awarness on environmental issues. They organize sustainable transportation days, carbon offsets and Earth day celebrations.

Girls Empowerment Club: This club organizes events and discussions on gender, feminism and current events.

Afterschool Academic Teams:

This is a great way for students to immerse themselves further in academics and gain a deeper understanding. Many of these teams partake in various tournaments competing against other schools and students

Debate Team: This team is a great way to utilize critical thinking and public speaking skills. You can learn and compete in various debate formats including public form, congress, imprompute and individual speaking. Debate Team meets twice a week (Monday & Wednesday) during the Fall and Winter trimesters.

Robotics Team: This team allows you to work efficiently with your teammates to build and code your own robot. You will compete in the FTC (First Tech Competition) with your teammates. Robotics Team meets twices a week (Thursdays & Fridays) during the Fall and Winter trimesters.

Chess Team: This team allows you to connect with other classmates who have a similar interest of playing chess. The chess team meets on Mondays afterschool and Thursdays during clubs time all year long.
