Here, you get to learn about all the exciting and engaging courses you can take as a 9th grader!

The following video discusses more about the EPS academic curriculum and courses.

Regular

Humanities Course Pairings:

Boundaries Literature & History: Explores boundaries within the self as well as in social and political aspects
Connections Literature & History: Explores connections within the identity and culture of the self and others
Exchange Literature & History: Focuses on exchange of power, knowledge and more between cultures and religions
Migration Literature & History: Focuses on the geographic migration, interaction and adaptation of people, ideas and cultures
Origins Literature & History: Delves into origin and development of culture, stories and early civilizations
Authority Literature & History: Delves into how authority is developed, maintained and eradicated in early civilizations

Other Academic Courses:

Biology: Science class which advances understanding on evolution, biochemistry, genetics, neuroscience and more
Spanish 1-4: Language class developing listening, speaking, reading and writing skills in Spanish
Math: Various levels ranging from Geometry to Calculus to enhance problem solving skills and application of math in the real world

Electives

Art electives:

Acting 1 & 2: Builds a basic foundation on performance skills such as vocal project, body moevemnts and text analysis
Stagecraft: Introduction on creating set design, props, costumes and makeup for productions
Vocal Rock Studio: Develops vocal and music-reading skills via the lens of a specific music era or genre
Rock Orchestra: Develops basic musician skills such as breathing, music theory and rhythm work through various instruments
Drawing and Painting 1 & 2: Explores traditional historical drawing paintin styles to create 2d art using various materials and surfaces
Digital Photography: Introduces students to capturing the world using photography and learning about the history and application of photography
Digital Filmmaking 1 & 2: Focuses on basic filmmaking and editing techniques
Graphic Design 1 & 2: Students work with design software to utilize both words and images to communicate ideas

Sports and Tech electives:

PE Wellness: Educates students on physical fitness, nutrition, mental health and more
PE: Customized Training to Maximize Performance Student customizes their own athletic experience to improve on their own fitness
Physical Education: Builds strength and agility through team sports and personal fitness plans
PE Yoga: Allows students to learn the basics of yoga by building strength and developing a mind-body connection
Programming 1 & 2 Learn elements like variables, loops and functions from languages like Java and Python
Web Design 1 & 2 Focuses on teaching students basic concepts to create their very own website
