Social events are a very important aspect of EPS culture, here are some annually occurring events.

Theater:

Each year, EPS directs three plays, one each trimester.

In the fall, the midde schoolers have the opportunity to audition. The winter trimester play is upper school, and the spring trimester is an all school musical. People from every grade are encouraged to participate. Additionally, EPS offers elective theater classes, so each student has the opportunity to explore theater in their classes.

Music:

EPS has many Choir and Music classes, there is a concert each trimester to showcase the work.

  • Upper school choir occurs during the usual block schedule. Their concert is the second to last one.
  • Chamber choir is the advanced choir, they practice from 7:30-8:15am, before usual classes begin. Their concert is the last one.
  • The instrumental music emsemble has the opportunity to play during the usual block schedule, they perform at the same time as the upper school choir.
  • Chamber music ensemble, like the Chamber choir, is the advanced ensemble and practices before school begins, they perform at the same time as the Chamber choir.

Art:

EPS offers art course during the day, both digital and physical. Art is hung around campus often, but it can also be seen at the art showcase that coincides with music concert.

Sports:

Each sport team has games or races that students and family are encouraged to attend. The frecency depends on the team - it can be multiple times a week to once every few months. In addition, some of our teams play at the homecoming or tailgate games. For more information about our teams, click here.

Social Events:

Each year, EPS holds homecoming, open to all upper school students. Prom occurs in the spring trimester and all 11-12 graders are encouraged to go.

In addition, there are some low-key social events that happen throughout the school year. For example, this year was a ice skating social.

EPS also has annual EBC weeks where students can travel to different states and explore.
