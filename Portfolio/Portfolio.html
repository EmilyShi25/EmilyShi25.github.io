My Progression in Sketching:

I started taking drawing lessons somewhere during freshman year. The first two pieces were basically me trying it out and learning about shapes. The third one I did is to help me understand the dimensions of the mouth and how light hits it. Its basically practice before drawing the actual mouth. The one after that is the same thing, understanding the shape of an eye and then actually drawing it out. I think the drawing of the eye is my best piece yet. Some struggles I had in the beginning was that all classes were online so I wasn't able to get as much guidance. The eye is the first piece I did in an actual studio. But there are also problems with drawing in a studio as well since the lighting and set up changes from time to time and its hard to get it to looked exactly like it did before.
