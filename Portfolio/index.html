时纾辰

Shuchen Shi: Shi Chen stands for time. Shi correlates with 时, Shu correlates with 纾, and Chen correlates with 辰

Intro:

I am currently a student in high school who likes photography, drawing, writing and coding.
I currently take classes for art and coding and take photos/edit films as a hobby

Art Style:

I currently only draw still life. My drawings usually take 1-2 months to complete.
This is because I like to be very intentional and meticulous with my strokes, causing the artwork to take a long time
One of the longest and best artworks would be a still life of an eye of a statue drawn with pencil.
I tend to only use one pencil, 6b, and use the pressure of my hands to control the shade.

My photography style is usually scenery. I rarely incorporate people and take these images using my phone.
When walking home through the different seasons, leaves and skys attract my attention and I take pictures to record them.

Writing:

I like to write stories that tries to invoke the emotions of the readers. I do so by triggering nostalgia and try to remember certain memories.
Often, these memories are tied to songs that I listen to, so I would flip through my old playlists.
